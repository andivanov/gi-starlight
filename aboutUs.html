We're a health insurance web broker.

If you’ve tried to buy health insurance anywhere else, you already know how overwhelming it can be — but we’re here to help. Our shopping tool makes it easy to find a plan you can afford. We can also help you apply for government money to pay for health insurance, if you qualify. And our licensed experts will answer all your Obamacare questions along the way.

We build health insurance marketplaces.

The very same technology that helps Americans shop for health insurance on GetInsured is also powering 4 state-based exchanges: California, New Mexico, Idaho, and Mississippi. Our SaaS, off-the-shelf platform powers insurance marketplaces for dozens of business partners, too.
